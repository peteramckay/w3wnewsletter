  • In a New York Times op-ed, University of Virginia professor Christopher Ali eloquently makese the case for a rural broadband plan in the U.S. akin to the New Deal's rural electification plan during the Great Depression. Amen!
  • Finally, unless you've been living under a rock, you've probably heard about the blockbuster Medium post by Amazon founder Jeff Bezos alleging blackmail by AMI, publisher of the National Enquirer. Of all the ink spilled on this, our favorite take so far is by author and business professor Scott Galloway, who took to Twitter to discuss the brouhaha's implications. Among them: It's a great reminder of the bullying and intimidation faced by other people on the internet with much fewer resources than Bezos, especially teenagers.
