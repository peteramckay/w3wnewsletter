### Looking Ahead

- **Mixed news for crypto in the U.S. Senate.** The Senate's crypto market structure bill, known as the Clarity Act, is set for a January markup, moving the landmark legislation closer to a floor vote. The bill aims to establish a comprehensive federal framework for digital assets by defining regulatory jurisdiction between the Securities and Exchange Commission and the Commodity Futures Trading Commission. However, Democratic concerns over regulatory independence and potential presidential power to appoint and remove agency commissioners may impact the bill's passage. ([Decrypt](https://decrypt.co/352981/senators-set-january-markup-for-crypto-market-structure-bill)) <!-- Draft summary by Leo/Llama 3.1 8B --> | Sen. Cynthia Lummis, a strong ally to the crypto industry in Washington, has announced she will not seek reelection when her term expires next year. Lummis, often referred to as the "Bitcoin Senator," has been instrumental in passing industry-favored legislation, including the GENIUS Act, which established a federal framework for stablecoins. Her retirement will likely impact ongoing negotiations over the crypto industry's coveted market structure bill.
