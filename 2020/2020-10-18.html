Now, let's get to the latest headlines:

  • Filecoin's main network went live on Thursday. In its official announcement of the milestone, the Filecoin project said it will host a speaker series starting Monday to discuss the new platform's potential to transform the web. (For full details, see https://tinyurl.com/y6jz9ol7) Filecoin has also launched a new video series on building Web 3.0 at https://tinyurl.com/y4hnsza4
  • Wall Street continues to warm toward bitcoin. Crypto-focused asset manager Grayscale Investments announced its saw more than $1 billion in investor inflows in the third quarter, a record. (See https://tinyurl.com/yxl4dn29) Stone Ridge Asset Management disclosed a bitcoin stake worth over $100 million. (See https://tinyurl.com/y4vmlxwq) Meanwhile, J.P. Morgan analysts hailed Square's recent decision to hold bitcoin in corporate treasury as "a strong vote of confidence" in the token. (See https://tinyurl.com/y58llvjv) And a new report from Fidelity recommended that investors consider allocating 5% of their portfolios to bitcoin for diversification. (See https://tinyurl.com/y28lcx4p)
  • The Bank of England asked banks on Monday how ready they are for zero or negative interest rates, following up its announcement last month that it was considering how to take rates below zero if necessary. (Reuters) Link: https://tinyurl.com/y6xhqhnf
  • CoinDesk's Daniel Palmer says it seems tennis legend Serena Williams' venture firm has sold its stake in Coinbase following the exchange's recent anti-activism policy for employees. Link: https://tinyurl.com/y2bwk3aq
  • Harvard's Nieman Lab reports on new research detailing two different approaches to fixing trust issues in journalism. There's the "traditional" way and the "engagement-oriented" way. (Hint: Only one of these two methods actually accounts for how the internet works.) Link: https://tinyurl.com/yydsd3nn
  • The web's inventor, Sir Tim Berners-Lee, detailed how his latest startup, Inrupt, is working with Belgium's Flanders region to give citizens there greater control of their data using semantic web technologies. Link: https://tinyurl.com/y4m5hfw9
  • The city of Toronto is going to start operating autonomous shuttle buses on a trial basis through an agreement with Local Motors beginning in Spring 2021. (TechCrunch) Link: https://tinyurl.com/y3kzjsrl
